Our Mission

To empower fantasy football enthusiasts—from casual players to seasoned analysts— with real-time, data-backed insights that make every roster decision confident and calculable.

How It Works

  1. Team Drafter Page: Fetches the top-10 available players at each position (with projected points, last season’s stats, injury risk) from the Fantasy Nerds Draft Rankings API. Click “Add Position” and lock in high-value picks instantly.
  2. Team Manager Page: Uses the Fantasy Nerds Daily Fantasy Football API to compare your current starter’s weekly projection against bench-player projections—so you can swap intelligently before kickoff.
  3. Lineup Optimization: Behind the scenes, we store your roster in Supabase and run machine-learning models to flag breakout candidates and avoid risky picks.

API Integration & Testing

  • Unit Tests: Verify REST/WebSocket requests and response parsing.
  • Integration Tests: Simulate full flows on both pages to confirm data displays correctly.
  • Load Tests: Use Apache JMeter to ensure sub-second responses even during peak NFL traffic.
